About Us

Dutch's Bait & Tackle is a small fishing retail shop that has been passed down for generations. Originally called Dutchs Bait & Tackle, Mr. Robert Vernitsky owned the bait shop at 4901 Ridge Ave and ran it for years until he could no more. It was then passed down to his first and only son, Bob Vernitsky in whiuch he called the store Bob's Bait & Tackle. Bob had a very successful business there until eventually the profits were close to none, he had to close his doors and sell the business. Fortunatly, a local man and his son were locals at this store and were ashamed to see it close it's doors for the final time. After a long and stressful winter, the two gentleman were able to buy out the business and the company in orer to live out the family legacy!!
